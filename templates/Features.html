This application, is designed to LOOK like a banking application,
but FUNCTIONS as an educational device,
to understand how system weaknesses and hacks work.

from Novice to educator, to current developers and techs alike.

we show the type of code, tools, and systems used,
we show the implications for potential victims,
AND want to strongly enforce the illegality of committing these attacks/hacks.
